ABOUT KITTIES TO GO

Kitties To Go was founded on one simple idea: humans deserve more purrs, more fluff, and more judgmental stares in their daily lives. Our team of highly trained felines works tirelessly to provide premium lap-warming services, dramatic meows, and occasional chaos.

Whether you need a cat to sit on your keyboard during an important meeting, knock pens off your desk for motivation, or simply nap in a sunbeam while you admire their beauty, we've got the perfect kitty for you. Our mission is to deliver top-tier feline companionship with just the right amount of attitude.

OUR MISSION

We strive to bring joy, comfort, and a little bit of mischief to every customer. Each cat is careful selected for maximum fluffiness, optimal purring volumn, and the ability to stare at you've done something wrong.

HOW IT ALL STARTED

Legend has it that our founder, Mr. Whiskerstein, leapt onto a car hood one rainy Seattle morning and refused to move until he received snacks. Inspired by his determination, we created a service where cats could share their charm with the world - one rental at a time.

Today, our fleet of fabulous felines continues that legacy by offering drive-thru purr therapy, emotional support chaos, and the occasional hairball (no extra charge).
