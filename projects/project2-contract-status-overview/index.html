Contract Status Overview

The Contract Status Overview is a simple webpage that organizes contract information into clear categories such as Open, Closed, and Under Review. It's designed to make it easy to understand the progress and status of each contract at a glance.

Why Contract Status Matters

Contracts go through different stages such as open, closed, and under review. Tracking these statuses helps teams stay organized, understand progress, and make sure nothing is missed. A clear overview makes it easier to follow up, meet deadlines, and keep work moving smoothly.

Fun Fact

Clear contract statuses help prevent missed deadlines and confusion.
